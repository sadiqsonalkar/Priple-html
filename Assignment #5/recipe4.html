Custard Fruit

Custard fruit is a sweet dish which is made up of milk, sugar and custard powder.Even without the fruits it taste awesome. And with fruits it taste heavenly. I like custard fruit because it is easy to make. It has no bad ingredients to affect our body. And it contains fruits which is good for health.Plus it is ready in no time.And when it is cool it is very tasty. So I like it very much.

I know how to make it so lets get started.

First we will see the ingredients to make custard fruit:

  • Custard powder
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Banana
  • mango
  • Chickoo

Now we will see in detail how to make custard fruit:

  1. First we will take a spoon of custard powder and mix it with cold milk. 🍌
  2. Then after mixing it with cold milk we will add that mixture to hot milk. 🍎
  3. Then we will add sugar as per taste. 🍏
  4. stir it till we see its thick and then we store it in refrigerator to cool it. 🍋
  5. After cooling it we will cut all the fruits and then mix it. 🍲
  6. Boom our recipe is ready. 🎉 🎊
